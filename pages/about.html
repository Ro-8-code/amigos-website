Our Story

Amigos  ·  Founded in Trearddur Bay

The beginning

Born between the sea
and the sombrero

Amigos was born in a room by the bay — in Trearddur Bay, Anglesey. A gathering place for good people, good waves, and the kind of conversations that last until sunrise.

The brand takes its name from the spirit of that room — the friends who filled it, the sea that surrounded it, and the feeling that some places on earth are simply irreplaceable.

Designed with love. Worn with purpose. Carried by the tide.

Trearddur Bay, Anglesey, Wales

The place

Trearddur Bay —
our corner of the world

On a small island off the north coast of Wales, where the Atlantic meets the Irish Sea, sits a bay that feels like it belongs to another world. Trearddur Bay — wild, beautiful, and quietly extraordinary.

The coordinates printed on every garment — 53°16'04" N, 4°37'35" W — are the exact location of where it all began. Every piece carries that place with it.

Trearddur Bay

The Place

Trearddur Bay sits on Holy Island off the north-west coast of Anglesey. Tidal pools, white sand, and a sky that changes every hour. It's where the brand lives — and breathes.

The Name

Amigos. Friends. The people who show up, who stay, who make a house feel like home. The brand is for them — and for anyone who's ever found their people in an unexpected place.

The Philosophy

A smooth sea never made a skilled sailor. We make clothes for people who embrace the storm — who know that the best stories come from the most unexpected moments.

The Future

Amigos is just beginning. Each piece is designed with intention — ethically made, built to last, and rooted in the wild beauty of the Welsh coast. Amigos por siempre.
