Trearddur Bay —
our corner of the world

On a small island off the north coast of Wales, where the Atlantic meets the Irish Sea, sits a bay that feels like it belongs to another world. Trearddur Bay — wild, beautiful, and quietly extraordinary.

It was here, in Trearddur Bay, that Amigos was born. A place of good people, sea air, and the kind of calm that only the ocean can bring.
